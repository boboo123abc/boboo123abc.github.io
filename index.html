Pancakes

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 6 Pancakes (2 Persons)
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Flour 100 g
Milk 300 ml
Oil a little bit

Step 1: Whisk the two egges.
Step 2:Add the flour and the milk and continue whisking, until smooth.
Step 3:Heat up a pan with a little bit of oil. Put the mixture in. Done!

Source: My mom

Pasta

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4 (2 Persons)
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200 g
Water 1.5 liter
Salt a pinch

Step 1:Bring the water to a boil.
Step 2:Add the spaghetti and let them cook for around 8 minutes.
Step 3:Take the spaghetti out of the pot and enjoy them with some parmesan or tomato sauce!
